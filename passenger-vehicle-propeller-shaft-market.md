<p><p>The passenger vehicle propeller shaft market consists of two main types: rigid shafts and hollow shafts. Rigid shafts are solid and offer enhanced durability and strength, making them suitable for high-performance vehicles. In contrast, hollow shafts are lightweight and provide better fuel efficiency, ideal for compact cars. The choice between these shafts often depends on vehicle design and performance requirements, influencing factors such as weight distribution, power transmission, and overall driving dynamics in various passenger vehicles.</p></p>
