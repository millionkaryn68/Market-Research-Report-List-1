<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Equipment Market includes various testing methods to evaluate material integrity without causing damage. Eddy Current Testing uses electromagnetic induction to detect surface and near-surface flaws. Magnetic Flux Leakage Testing identifies irregularities in ferromagnetic materials by measuring the magnetic field. Ultrasonic Testing employs high-frequency sound waves to detect internal defects. Additionally, the "Other" category encompasses techniques such as radiographic testing and visual inspection, offering diverse solutions for maintenance and quality assurance across industries.</p></p>
