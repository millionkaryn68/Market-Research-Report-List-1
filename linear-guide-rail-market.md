Ball linear guide rails utilize balls for smooth motion and are ideal for high-speed applications. Roller linear guide rails use cylindrical rollers for higher load capacities and increased stability. Needle linear guide rails employ needle bearings for compact and precise movement in tight spaces. The "others" category encompasses specialized or niche guide rail systems tailored to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
