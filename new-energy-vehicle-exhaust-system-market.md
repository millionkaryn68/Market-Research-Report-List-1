<p><ul><li>Single Exhaust System</li><li>Dual Exhaust System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) exhaust system market includes single and dual exhaust systems. A single exhaust system uses one pipe to expel gases, offering cost efficiency and simplicity, ideal for compact NEVs. Dual exhaust systems feature two pipes, enhancing performance by reducing backpressure and improving engine efficiency, often used in high-performance or larger NEVs. Both systems are designed to minimize emissions, aligning with NEV sustainability goals, but differ in complexity, cost, and application based on vehicle requirements.</p></p>
