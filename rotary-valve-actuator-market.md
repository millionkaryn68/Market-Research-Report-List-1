<p><p>The Rotary Valve Actuator market is classified into several categories based on the power source. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to operate valves, offering rapid response and simplicity. Hydraulic actuators utilize fluid pressure for higher force applications, ideal for heavy-duty settings. Electric actuators convert electrical energy into mechanical motion, providing precise control and easy integration with automation systems. Each type serves distinct industries and operational needs.</p></p>
