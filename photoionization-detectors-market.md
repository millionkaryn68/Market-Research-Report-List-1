<p><p>The photoionization detectors (PIDs) market comprises two main types: single photoionization detectors and multi photoionization detectors. Single PIDs are designed to detect specific gases by using a single light source, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for targeted applications. In contrast, multi PIDs utilize multiple light sources to simultaneously detect a broader range of substances, providing versatility and enhanced sensitivity. Both types are crucial in environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and hazardous material detection, catering to different operational needs and complexities.</p></p>
