<p><p>The Automotive Advanced Suspension System market includes two main types: Independent and Non-independent systems. Independent Automotive Suspension Systems allow each wheel to move independently, enhancing ride comfort, handling, and stability, especially on uneven surfaces. Non-independent Automotive Suspension Systems connect wheels on the same axle, offering simpler design and cost-effectiveness but with reduced ride comfort and handling precision. Both systems cater to diverse vehicle needs, balancing performance, durability, and cost for various automotive applications.</p></p>
