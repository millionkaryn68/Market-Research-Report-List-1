<p><p>The spray washing machines market is primarily divided into two types: top loader and front loader machines. Top loader machines allow users to load clothes from the top, offering convenience and faster wash cycles. They typically use more water and are easier to load. In contrast, front loader machines are more water and energy efficient, utilizing a horizontal drum design that provides superior cleaning by tumbling clothes through water. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and laundry needs.</p></p>
