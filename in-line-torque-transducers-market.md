Static torque sensors measure torque during stationary conditions, making them ideal for applications like assembly and quality control. In contrast, dynamic torque sensors assess torque in real-time during rotational motion, suitable for applications like engine testing and machinery performance. Both types are essential for precision engineering and monitoring in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
