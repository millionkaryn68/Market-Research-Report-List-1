<p><p>The cell counting spectrophotometer market comprises two main types: single-mode readers and multi-mode readers. Single-mode readers are specialized instruments designed for specific applications, providing accurate readings for cell concentration and viability using a single method. In contrast, multi-mode readers offer versatility by supporting multiple measurement techniques, such as absorbance, fluorescence, and luminescence. This flexibility caters to a broader range of research needs, making multi-mode readers popular in various laboratory settings where diverse analytical capabilities are required.</p></p>
