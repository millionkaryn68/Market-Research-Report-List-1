<p><p>Railway electrification systems encompass various components essential for the efficient operation of electric trains. Traction power supply systems convert and distribute electrical energy to these contact lines, ensuring consistent voltage and frequency for train operation. Other market types include auxiliary systems, such as signaling and communication infrastructure, which support overall railway functionality. Together, these systems enhance efficiency, reduce emissions, and improve operational reliability in rail transport.</p></p>
