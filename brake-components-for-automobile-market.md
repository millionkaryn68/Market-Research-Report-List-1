<p><p>The automobile brake components market includes various critical parts essential for vehicle braking systems. Brake pads are friction materials that press against rotors to slow down or stop vehicles. Brake shoes perform a similar function but are used in drum brakes. Brake lining is a friction material for internal brake components. Brake rotors, typically disc-shaped, dissipate heat generated during braking. Brake drums enclose the brake shoes and are crucial in drum braking systems. Additional components include various hardware and sensors that enhance braking performance.</p></p>
