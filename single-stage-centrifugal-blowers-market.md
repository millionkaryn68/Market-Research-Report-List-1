<p><p>Single-stage centrifugal blowers are categorized into low-speed and high-speed markets based on operating speeds and applications. Low-speed blowers typically operate at under 5,000 RPM, offering high efficiency and quiet operation, making them suitable for ventilation and industrial processes that require moderate pressure. In contrast, high-speed blowers operate above 5,000 RPM, delivering greater airflow and pressure, ideal for applications in food processing, wastewater treatment, and pneumatic conveying. The choice between them depends on performance requirements and energy efficiency needs.</p></p>
