<p><p>The Milk Ingredients Analyzing System Market is segmented into two main types: Ultrasonic Analyzing Systems and Infrared Analyzing Systems. Infrared Systems employ light absorption techniques to determine compositional elements such as lactose and solids. Both technologies are essential for dairy manufacturers to ensure product quality, optimize processes, and comply with regulatory standards in the competitive dairy industry.</p></p>
