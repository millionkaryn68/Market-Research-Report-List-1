<p><p>The Battery Management and Protection System (BMPS) market comprises two main types: decentralized and centralized systems. Decentralized systems manage individual battery cells independently, enhancing overall safety and performance by allowing more precise monitoring and control. In contrast, centralized systems monitor and manage the entire battery pack from a single point, simplifying design and reducing costs. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing battery life, ensuring safety, and improving efficiency in applications like electric vehicles and renewable energy storage.</p></p>
