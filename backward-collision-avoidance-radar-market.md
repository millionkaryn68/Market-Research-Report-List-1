<p><p>Backward Collision Avoidance Radar (BCAR) systems enhance vehicle safety by preventing collisions when reversing. There are three main types: </p><p>1. **Ultrasonic Radar** uses sound waves to detect obstacles within a short range, ideal for parking scenarios.</p><p>2. **Laser Radar** employs laser beams for precise distance measurement, providing high accuracy and longer range detection.</p><p>3. **Microwave Radar** utilizes radio waves, offering a broader detection range under various environmental conditions and capable of identifying multiple obstacles simultaneously. Each type serves unique applications in vehicle safety systems.</p></p>
