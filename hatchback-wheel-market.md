<p><p>The hatchback wheel market can be divided into two main types: casting and forging. Casting wheels are created by pouring molten metal into a mold, allowing for complex designs and cost-effectiveness, making them popular among mass-produced vehicles. In contrast, forging involves shaping heated metal under high pressure for enhanced strength and durability, often favored for performance-oriented hatchbacks. Each type caters to different consumer needs, balancing factors like weight, performance, cost, and aesthetics in automotive design.</p></p>
