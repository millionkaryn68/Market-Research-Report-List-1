<p><p>The PVDC active food packaging market consists of two primary types: PVDC latex and PVDC resin. PVDC latex is used to produce flexible packaging films that enhance barrier properties, providing excellent protection against moisture, gases, and odors. This type is often used in food applications to extend shelf life. Meanwhile, PVDC resin is utilized in rigid packaging solutions, offering similar barrier advantages but in a more robust form. Both types contribute to preserving food quality and reducing waste.</p></p>
