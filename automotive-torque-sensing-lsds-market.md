<p><p>The automotive torque sensing limited-slip differentials (LSDs) market includes various types, such as cone-type LSDs, plate-type LSDs, and others. Cone-type LSDs use conical friction surfaces to transfer torque between wheels, enhancing traction, especially in off-road applications. Plate-type LSDs, on the other hand, utilize clutch plates to provide improved grip during cornering and acceleration. Other types may include electronic LSDs and viscous coupling LSDs, each offering distinct advantages in performance and handling across different vehicle platforms.</p></p>
