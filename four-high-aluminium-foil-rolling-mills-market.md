<p><p>The Four-High Aluminium Foil Rolling Mills market comprises several key types: Roughing Mills, Intermediate Mills, and Finishing Mills. Roughing Mills initially reduce the thickness of aluminum slabs, preparing them for further processing. Intermediate Mills refine the material's thickness and surface characteristics, enhancing quality and uniformity. Finishing Mills conduct final adjustments, ensuring precision and optimal surface finish for foil products. Together, these mill types play a crucial role in producing high-quality aluminum foil for various applications in packaging and other industries.</p></p>
