<p><p>Reconstituted bamboo refers to processed bamboo fibers used in various products, with two primary market types: cold pressing and hot pressing. Cold pressing involves compressing bamboo fibers at lower temperatures, preserving natural properties and resulting in durable, eco-friendly materials suitable for flooring and furniture. Hot pressing, on the other hand, utilizes high temperatures and pressure, leading to denser, stronger products often used in construction and high-impact applications. Both methods cater to growing demand for sustainable alternatives in various industries.</p></p>
