<p><p>The medical electric endoscopic stapler market is categorized into three main types: linear, standard, and circular staplers. Linear staplers are designed for creating straight, linear rows of staples, ideal for procedures requiring tissue resection. Standard staplers are versatile and used for various surgical applications, while circular staplers provide a continuous suture line, essential for anastomosis in tubular structures. Each type offers unique functionalities tailored to specific surgical needs, contributing to advancements in minimally invasive surgical techniques.</p></p>
