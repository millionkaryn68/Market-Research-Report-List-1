<p><p>The infrared flammable gas detector market comprises two main types: Point IR Detectors and Open Path IR Detectors. Point IR Detectors are designed to monitor specific locations for gas leaks by detecting infrared radiation from gases within a defined area, providing precise measurements. In contrast, Open Path IR Detectors cover larger areas by analyzing the infrared radiation along a predetermined path, enabling the detection of gas leaks over extended distances. Both types are essential for ensuring safety in industrial settings.</p></p>
