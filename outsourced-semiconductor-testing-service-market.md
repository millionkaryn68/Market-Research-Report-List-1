Wafer testing involves assessing the performance of semiconductor wafers before they are diced into individual chips, ensuring they meet quality standards. IC testing, on the other hand, focuses on evaluating the functionality and reliability of integrated circuits after packaging. Both types are crucial for maintaining product integrity and performance, driving demand within the semiconductor industry for efficient and reliable testing services.</p></p>
