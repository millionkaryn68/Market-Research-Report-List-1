Single-side serrated nuts feature serrations on one face, providing effective locking and stability in applications where space is limited. Conversely, double-sided serrated nuts have serrations on both faces, enhancing grip and resistance to loosening, making them ideal for more dynamic or high-stress environments. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, ensuring optimal performance and reliability in various mechanical assemblies.</p></p>
