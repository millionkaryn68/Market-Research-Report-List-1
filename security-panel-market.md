<p><p>The security panel market is divided into two primary types: wired and wireless systems. Wired security panels use physical connections to transmit signals, offering reliability and often higher resistance to interference. They are typically installed in existing structures and require more extensive installation efforts. Conversely, wireless security panels operate without cables, providing flexibility and easier installation, making them ideal for both new and retrofitted systems. Wireless panels rely on radio frequencies, making them more versatile but potentially susceptible to signal disruptions.</p></p>
