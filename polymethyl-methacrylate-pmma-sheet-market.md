<p><strong>The Poly(methyl methacrylate) (PMMA) Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Poly(methyl methacrylate) (PMMA) Sheet</li><li>Extruded Poly(methyl methacrylate) (PMMA) Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PMMA sheet market is primarily divided into two types: cast PMMA sheets and extruded PMMA sheets. Cast PMMA sheets are produced by polymerizing methyl methacrylate in a mold, resulting in high optical clarity and superior surface quality, making them ideal for applications like signage and displays. In contrast, extruded PMMA sheets are created through continuous extrusion, offering lower cost and faster production rates. These are widely used in applications such as glazing and lighting fixtures, emphasizing versatility in various industries.</p></p>
