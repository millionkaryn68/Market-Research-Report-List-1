<p><p>The pond liner market is characterized by various materials, each with unique properties. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) liners are cost-effective and flexible, making them popular for small to medium ponds. Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) offers excellent durability and elasticity, ideal for larger installations. Butyl rubber provides superior waterproofing, while polyurea is highly resistant to chemicals and UV rays, suitable for industrial applications. Polyester and polyethylene liners are lightweight and versatile, catering to diverse pond designs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
