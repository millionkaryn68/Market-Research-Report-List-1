<p><p>The Automotive Keyless Go market includes Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows vehicle access and ignition start without physically using a key, relying on proximity sensors to detect the key fob. RKES requires manual interaction, such as pressing a button on the key fob to unlock or start the car. Both systems enhance convenience and security, with PKES offering a more seamless, hands-free experience, while RKES provides straightforward, user-controlled functionality.</p></p>
