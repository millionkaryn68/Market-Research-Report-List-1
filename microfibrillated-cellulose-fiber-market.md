<p><p>The Microfibrillated Cellulose (MFC) Fiber Market comprises two key types: Barrier Films and Performance Enhancers. Barrier films utilize MFC for their excellent gas and moisture resistance, making them suitable for packaging applications. These films enhance the shelf life of products by preventing spoilage. On the other hand, Performance Enhancers incorporate MFC into various materials to improve mechanical properties, tensile strength, and durability. Together, these applications of MFC support a range of industries, including food packaging and construction.</p></p>
