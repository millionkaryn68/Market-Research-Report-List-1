<p><p>The stainless steel safety valve market comprises types such as hydraulic and pneumatic safety valves. Hydraulic safety valves regulate pressure in fluid systems using hydraulic force, ensuring safe operation under varying conditions. Pneumatic safety valves, on the other hand, utilize compressed air to control pressure and release excess, making them ideal for gas systems. Both types are crucial in preventing overpressure scenarios, enhancing safety and reliability in various industrial applications, including oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and chemical processing.</p></p>
