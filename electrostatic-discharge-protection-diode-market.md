<p><strong>The Electrostatic Discharge Protection Diode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unidirectional ESD Protection Diode</li><li>Bidirectional ESD Protection Diode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Protection Diode market consists of two primary types: unidirectional and bidirectional ESD protection diodes. Unidirectional ESD protection diodes are designed to conduct current in one direction, effectively clamping voltage spikes to protect sensitive components from single-polarity ESD events. In contrast, bidirectional ESD protection diodes can handle voltage spikes in both directions, making them suitable for applications where signals can oscillate between positive and negative polarities. Both types are essential for safeguarding electronic devices in various industries.</p></p>
