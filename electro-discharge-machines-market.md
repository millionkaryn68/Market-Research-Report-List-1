<p><p>The electro-discharge machines (EDM) market primarily comprises two main types: electric spark forming machines and EDM wire cutting machines. Electric spark forming machines utilize controlled electrical discharges to shape and mold materials with precision, making them ideal for complex geometries and detailed components. EDM wire cutting machines, on the other hand, employ a fine metallic wire to cut through conductive materials, allowing for intricate shapes and high accuracy. Both types are essential in industries requiring precision engineering, such as aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
