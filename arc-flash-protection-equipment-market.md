<p><p>Arc flash protection equipment is categorized into Hazard Risk Category (HRC) levels that indicate the potential risk of arc flash hazards and the corresponding protective gear. HRC 1 includes minimal protection, suitable for low-risk environments with a maximum incident energy of 1.2 cal/cm². HRC 2 offers moderate protection for risks up to 8 cal/cm². HRC 3 gear protects against higher risks, typically up to 25 cal/cm², while HRC 4 is designed for the most hazardous conditions, protecting against incidents exceeding 40 cal/cm².</p></p>
