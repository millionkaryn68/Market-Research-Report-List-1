<p><p>The aerospace market for special fibers encompasses two primary types: conductive fibers and insulation fibers. Conductive fibers are designed to provide electromagnetic shielding and static dissipation, essential for protecting sensitive electronic systems in aircraft. Insulation fibers, on the other hand, focus on thermal and acoustic insulation, enhancing passenger comfort and safety by managing heat and noise. Both types contribute significantly to the performance, safety, and efficiency of aerospace applications, meeting stringent industry standards and technological advancements.</p></p>
