<p><strong>The Karl Fischer Titrators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulometric Titration</li><li>Volumetric Titration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Karl Fischer titrator market is segmented into two primary types: coulometric titration and volumetric titration. Coulometric titration is ideal for measuring low moisture content, utilizing a constant current to produce iodine in situ, making it suitable for small sample sizes. In contrast, volumetric titration measures moisture by adding a titrant solution until a reaction endpoint is reached, allowing for larger sample quantities. Both methods serve critical roles in industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals, ensuring accurate moisture analysis.</p></p>
