Phosphates, derived from phosphoric acid, provide effective flame retardancy and thermal stability, commonly used in plastics, textiles, and coatings. Phosphites, on the other hand, serve as both stabilizers and flame retardants, enhancing the performance of materials while reducing flammability. Both types are increasingly favored due to their efficiency and environmental benefits, driving growth in industries focused on safety and sustainability.</p></p>
