<p><p>The Automotive Vacuum Brake Booster market includes two main types: Single Diaphragm and Double Diaphragm boosters. Single Diaphragm boosters use one diaphragm to amplify braking force, making them compact and cost-effective, suitable for smaller vehicles. Double Diaphragm boosters feature two diaphragms, providing higher braking force and efficiency, ideal for larger or heavier vehicles requiring enhanced braking performance. Both types rely on engine vacuum to assist braking, but Double Diaphragm designs offer greater power and reliability for demanding applications.</p></p>
