<p><p>The ceramic catalyst substrate market is primarily divided into two types: cordierite and silicon carbide materials. Cordierite substrates are known for their thermal stability, lightweight properties, and excellent thermal shock resistance, making them ideal for automotive applications. In contrast, silicon carbide substrates offer superior thermal conductivity and mechanical strength, suitable for high-performance catalytic converters. Both materials play a crucial role in enhancing exhaust gas purification, contributing to environmental sustainability and meeting stringent emission regulations in various industries.</p></p>
