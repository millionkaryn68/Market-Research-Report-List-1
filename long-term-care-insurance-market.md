<p><p>Traditional Long-Term Care Insurance (LTCI) is a standalone policy designed to cover long-term care expenses, such as nursing homes or in-home care, with premiums paid regularly. If care is not needed, the policyholder receives no benefits. Hybrid LTCI combines long-term care coverage with life insurance or annuities, offering a death benefit or cash value if care is unused. Hybrid policies often have higher upfront costs but provide flexibility and potential returns, appealing to those seeking dual-purpose coverage.</p></p>
