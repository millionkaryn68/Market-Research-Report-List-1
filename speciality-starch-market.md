<p><p>The specialty starch market is categorized by granule size into three primary types: large, medium, and small granule sizes. Large granule starches are typically used in applications requiring thicker consistency, such as sauces and gravies. Medium granule starches offer versatility for various food products, providing stability and texture. Small granule starches are ideal for instant foods and bakery items, as they dissolve quickly, ensuring smoothness. Each size serves distinct functional purposes across diverse industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
