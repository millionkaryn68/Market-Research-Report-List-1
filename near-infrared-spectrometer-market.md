<p><p>The Near Infrared Spectrometer market is categorized into several types: FT-NIR (Fourier Transform Near Infrared) utilizes broad-spectrum light for detailed molecular analysis, ideal for complex samples. UV-Vis-NIR combines ultraviolet and visible light with near-infrared for a wide range of applications, particularly in quality control. Raman spectroscopy, leveraging inelastic scattering of light, provides unique molecular fingerprints, useful in chemical identification. Other types may include hybrid spectrometers and specialized instruments for niche applications, catering to diverse industries such as pharmaceuticals and food safety.</p></p>
