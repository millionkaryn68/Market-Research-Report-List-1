<p><strong>The Refractive Modulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractive modulator market is segmented into Type I and Type II categories based on operational principles and applications. Type I modulators utilize variable refractive index materials to control phase shifts in light, primarily for telecommunications and data transmission. In contrast, Type II modulators employ electro-optic or thermally-responsive materials, offering enhanced control over light intensity and frequency, suitable for advanced imaging and display technologies. Both types are vital in improving optical communication and enhancing display quality in various devices.</p></p>
